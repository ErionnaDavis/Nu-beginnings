Profile:
Marilyn Parker specializes in providing employment, career and educational service opportunities and is dedicated to serving communities and worship centers in the Greater St. Louis Metropolitan and Illinois areas.
Focus:
Ms. Parker has organized over 40 educational and employment events geared toward job promotion, placement and retention. Making the public aware of career and occupational fields as well as encouraging the pursuit of accreditations, certifications and degrees. She has touched the lives of over 5,500 individuals. During a previous event, 36 participants were hired on the spot! Her employment ministry is an evangelism tool that provides the opportunity to share employment resources and the
Good News about Jesus Christ
