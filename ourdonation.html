General Donations

As a nonprofit organization, our future goals of assisting the community relies upon contributions, donations, and charitable gifts. This helps us to continue our missions of lowering the unemployment rate and for providing hope and encouragement to every unemployed and underemployed person in America.

Computer Center Donations

Many of our clients and visitors do not have funds to pay for our products or services and in turn, Nu Beginnings waves their service charges and provide the sponsorship for their employment pursuit. For every $100 received, Nu Beginnings will provide employment services for 4 unemployed individuals. Each week, our client base is increasing, and to remain a reliable resource for employment assistance,we must expand our office.
